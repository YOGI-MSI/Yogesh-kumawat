Information About Mars

Mars is known as the "Red Planet" because of the reddish color of its surface. It is home to the tallest volcano, Olympus Mons, and the largest canyon, Valles Marineris. Mars has seasons, polar ice caps, and evidence of ancient water flows.

About Mars Exploration

Exploration of Mars has been a significant focus for space agencies worldwide. Rovers like Perseverance and Curiosity have been sent to collect data about the planet's geology, climate, and potential for life.
